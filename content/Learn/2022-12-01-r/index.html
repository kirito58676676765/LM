因子型：factor()生成

a = factor(c('man','female','man'))
a
## [1] man    female man   
## Levels: female man
#上述等价于
(a = factor(c('man','female','man'))) #赋值的同时运行变量。
## [1] man    female man   
## Levels: female man
#同样可以对因子水平进行排序
(b = factor(c('man','female','man'),ordered = T)) #按英文首字母顺序排序
## [1] man    female man   
## Levels: female < man
(c = factor(c(1,2,3),ordered = T)) #按数值大小排序
## [1] 1 2 3
## Levels: 1 < 2 < 3
#因子型的转换
all = c(1,2,3);all
## [1] 1 2 3
as.factor(all)
## [1] 1 2 3
## Levels: 1 2 3
